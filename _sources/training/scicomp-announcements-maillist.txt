=====================
Announcement maillist
=====================

Events and other Aalto Scientific Computing (Science-IT) announcements
distributed over several lists like Triton users, departments. In addition
we run *scicomp-announcements@list.aalto.fi* maillist that covers
everyone else who wants to stay tuned and recieve Science IT news.

The moderated list is free to subscribe / unsubsribe at any time,
accepts all emails including non-Aalto ones.

 * `scicomp-announcements web page for users <https://list.aalto.fi/mailman/listinfo/scicomp-announcements>`__
