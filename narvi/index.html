Narvi user guide¶

Narvi is the Tampere Universities high-performance computing cluster. It serves all researchers of universitys, but is currently coordinated from within the Tampere Center of Scientific Computing. It is similar to the CSC clusters, though CSC clusters are larger and Narvi is easier to use because it is more integrated into the local environment.
